This report it used to track some ideas and some projects of a digital area.

For Ideas, it's a place that the client came and fill in some informations of some pain point that he got. Then, it is shown in the report to the digtal area analyse if it makes sense and if they can help.

Once they approve those ideas, it will become a project (Projects Screens). There the digital team inputs informations about project phases, milestones and progress of each activities. The report bring all the information in a friendly form, that the client can check how is the progress of his project.

All the information are inputed in a canvas Power App (.zip of the folder)
